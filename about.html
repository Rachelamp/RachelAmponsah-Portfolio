About

Anyekai is a passionate and innovative artist, software engineer and recent graduate from Kennesaw State University with a focus on blending music and technology. As a vocalist and aspiring multi-instrumentalist, Anyekai has developed a deep connection to music, honing skills in guitar, bass, mandolin, and performance. With a background in software engineering and experience in SonicPi, SuperCollider, and web design, Anyekai brings a technical edge to creative pursuits. Driven by a commitment to pushing boundaries, Anyekai explores projects that merge emotional storytelling, interactive audience experiences, and cutting-edge music technology. From rebuilding a Stratocaster-style guitar to planning to create groundbreaking software for live performances, Anyekai is dedicated to crafting innovative and meaningful work that connects with audiences on a deeper level.
